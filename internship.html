Company / Organization Details

Organization: Valenzuela City Government Employees Cooperative (VCGEC)

Established: December 23, 1991

Registration: Cooperative Development Authority (CDA) No. MLA-26211

Purpose: Encouraging savings and providing productive loan services to members; supporting education, livelihood, and community programs.

Vision: Stable and progressive life towards abundancy.

Mission: Enabling members to improve their standard of living through quality products and services and institutionalizing social programs on education and livelihood.

Core Values: Vibrant, Committed, Good Governance, Equality, Compassion

Internship Job Description / Assigned Tasks

  • Assist in daily administrative operations and documentation.
  • Support loan processing and member record keeping.
  • Help with community program activities.
  • Coordinate with staff to collect, organize, and file member data.
  • Participate in team meetings and cooperative events when needed.
